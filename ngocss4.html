About Us

Reyyah is committed to creating lasting change through education, healthcare, and women empowerment. we believe in unplifting communities by investing in people's potential.

We envision a world where every child receives an education, every woman has a voice, and every family has access to healthcare. Our mission is to break cycles of poverty by empowering indivisuals with the tools and opportunities to thrive.

Our work

Education

We provide access to quality education and learning oppurtunities for underprivileged children

Women Empowerment

We support women with skills training, mentorship and resourcrs to lead independent lives.

Healthcare

We run health camps, awareness programs and provide basic medical support in underserved areas.
